Kylian Mbappe

Kylian Mbappe is a French professional footballer who plays as a forward for Paris Saint-Germain and the France national team. Kylian Mbappe was born on December 20, 1998, in Bondy, France. He began his football career at a young age and quickly rose through the ranks of youth football in France. He made his professional debut for AS Monaco in 2015 at the age of 16, becoming the youngest player to score a goal in Ligue 1. In 2017, he transferred to Paris Saint-Germain for a record fee of €180 million, making him the second most expensive footballer in history. Mbappe has won numerous awards and accolades throughout his career, including the Golden Boot in 2019 and 2020. He has also been named the Ligue 1 Player of the Year and has been included in the UEFA Team of the Year. Mbappe is known for his speed, skill, and goal-scoring ability, and is considered one of the best young players in the world.

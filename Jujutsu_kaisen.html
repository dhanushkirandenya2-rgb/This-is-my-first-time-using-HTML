Plot


In a world where negative human emotions manifest as deadly Cursed Spirits, high schooler Yuji Itadori accidentally swallows a rotting finger belonging to Ryomen Sukuna—the legendary King of Curses.

To contain the curse, Yuji is thrust into the secretive world of Tokyo Jujutsu High under the mentorship of Satoru Gojo, forcing him into a lethal battle to consume the remaining fingers before his eventual execution.
